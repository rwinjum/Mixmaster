🎵 MIXMASTER Instructions

DJ Playlist Organizer - Step-by-Step Guide

📋 Overview

MIXMASTER is a powerful DJ playlist organizer that uses audio analysis to create balanced, mixed playlists. It intelligently groups songs based on their musical properties (energy, tempo, danceability, etc.) to create smooth transitions and maintain consistent pacing.

🚀 Quick Start: 3 Main Steps

1Get Your Spotify Playlist Data

Export your Spotify playlist with full audio metadata using Exportify:

  1. Go to https://exportify.app
  2. Sign in with your Spotify account
  3. Select your playlist (e.g., "BW26")
  4. Click "Export" and download the CSV file
✅ The exported CSV will include all audio features: Danceability, Energy, Tempo, etc.

2Upload Your CSV

Import your playlist data into MIXMASTER:

  1. Click the upload zone (or drag & drop)
  2. Select your exported CSV file
  3. Wait for the file to load (you'll see a success message)
  4. Check the Song Count to confirm all tracks loaded
💡 The CSV parser automatically detects Spotify export format and maps columns correctly.

3Organize Your Playlist

Create balanced, mixed playlists using our algorithms:

Option A: Group of Three (Recommended)

  1. Click "🎵 Organize Playlist"
  2. Select "Group of Three" from the dropdown
  3. Click "Organize"
  4. See your songs grouped into balanced sets of 3

Option B: Energy-Based Organization

  1. Select "By Energy" from the dropdown
  2. Organizes songs from low → high energy
  3. Great for building energy through a set

Option C: By Artist

  1. Select "By Artist"
  2. Groups all songs by artist
  3. Useful for themed DJ sets

📊 Understanding the Results

After organizing, each group displays:

  • Position: Where the song falls in the group
  • Song Info: Track name, artist, album
  • Audio Features: BPM, Energy, Danceability, etc.
  • Group Label: Shows the grouping strategy and properties

🤖 How MIXMASTER Works

MIXMASTER combines intelligent algorithms with optional AI analysis to create the perfect DJ playlist:

The Core Engine

  1. Data Import: You export your Spotify playlist with full audio metadata using Exportify
  2. Rule-Based Grouping: MIXMASTER's algorithm analyzes 12 audio features and groups songs based on your selected rules:
    • By Genre: Groups similar music styles together
    • By Era: Groups songs from the same time period
    • By Pace: Groups by energy and tempo for flow control
    • Combined Rules: Mix multiple criteria for sophisticated organization
  3. AI Enhancement (Optional): When Ollama is running, AI analyzes each group's musical compatibility, considering:
    • Tempo transitions between songs
    • Key compatibility (harmonic mixing)
    • Energy progression
    • Genre and era cohesion
  4. Smart Optimization: The system ensures artist variety and smooth transitions
💡 Tech Stack: MIXMASTER uses HTML5/JavaScript frontend, Python Flask backend, Spotify API for metadata, and optional Ollama AI integration for compatibility analysis.

⭐ Understanding AI Ratings

Each group of 3 songs receives an AI Compatibility Score that evaluates how well they work together:

🟢 8-10 Excellent
Perfect flow and compatibility. Seamless transitions between songs.
🟡 6-7 Good
Solid grouping with good compatibility. Minor adjustment opportunities.
🔴 5 or Below
Algorithmic grouping only. (Ollama not running or AI unavailable)

What the AI Analyzes

  • Tempo Compatibility: How smoothly BPM transitions between songs
  • Key Matching: Harmonic compatibility for smooth mixing
  • Energy Progression: Does energy build, drop, or stay consistent?
  • Genre Flow: How well do genres complement each other?
  • Era Diversity: Does the group have interesting era combinations?
⚡ To Get AI Ratings: Make sure Ollama is running on your system. MIXMASTER will automatically use AI analysis to generate compatibility scores when available. Without Ollama, you'll get rule-based grouping (score 5/10) but the organization is still musically sound.

❓ FAQ

Q: Why do I need audio features?

Audio features enable intelligent grouping. Without them, MIXMASTER can't analyze what makes songs work together. Use Exportify to get complete data.

Q: Can I use songs without Spotify data?

Yes, but you'll get less intelligent grouping. MIXMASTER works best with full audio metadata from Exportify.

Q: How many songs can I organize?

MIXMASTER handles playlists of any size, from 10 to 500+ songs. Performance stays smooth throughout.

Q: Can I edit the groups after organizing?

Currently, grouping is automatic. You can re-organize with different strategies or remix to try again.

Q: How do I use this for DJing?

Export to Spotify, download to your DJ software, or use the CSV data. The "Group of Three" creates smooth song transitions for live sets.

🔧 Troubleshooting

⚠️ CSV Won't Upload?
Make sure your CSV is from Exportify and has all columns (Track name, Artist, audio features). If it still fails, try opening and re-saving in Excel first.
⚠️ No Audio Features Showing?
Use Exportify instead of TuneMyMusic. TuneMyMusic doesn't include audio features in its export.
💡 Tip: Save your organized playlists! You can always reload the original CSV to try different grouping strategies.

📞 Getting Help

For issues or feature requests, check that you're using Exportify to export your playlist with all audio features included. This solves most problems!
